Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pricing and plans

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept ACH, wire transfer, and major credit cards. Enterprise customers can also arrange invoicing with NET 30 terms.
What are the contract terms?
Professional and Enterprise tiers require a 12-month commitment. Enterprise Plus contracts are typically 24-36 months. Month-to-month options available at 20% premium.
Can we add users mid-contract?
Absolutely! You can add users at any time. Additional users are prorated and billed on your next invoice at your current per-user rate.
Do you offer volume discounts?
Yes! Volume discounts: 25-49 users (10% off) | 50-99 users (15% off) | 100+ users (20% off). Custom discounts available for 200+ users.
Can we try before we buy?
We offer a 30-day free trial for up to 5 users with full access to Professional tier features. Request a personalized demo to get started.
Is Harken CRE secure enough for banks?
Yes! We're SOC 2 Type II certified with bank-grade encryption (AES-256), role-based access control, comprehensive audit logs, and regular security audits. All data is stored in secure, redundant AWS data centers in the US.
What's your uptime guarantee?
Standard SLA is 99.5% uptime for all plans. Enterprise Plus includes 99.9% SLA with financial penalties for downtime exceeding the guarantee.
Is training included?
Yes! Professional tier includes 2 hours, Enterprise includes 4 hours, and Enterprise Plus includes unlimited training. Additional sessions available at $500/session.
